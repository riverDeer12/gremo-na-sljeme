Gremo na Sljeme - Day 1

  • 8:00 - 00:00 Jan 24, 2024
  • Podsljeme, Zagreb
40.00 €

08:00 - 12:00: Morning Mountain Vibes

Begin your day with the crisp mountain air and breathtaking views as you hit the slopes for morning skiing and snowboarding sessions. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a first-timer, the slopes of Sljeme offer the perfect terrain for all skill levels.

12:30 - 14:00: Eats and Treats

Recharge with a variety of delicious alpine-inspired dishes from local vendors. Warm up with hearty soups, sip on hot cocoa, and indulge in sweet treats, all while enjoying the lively atmosphere of the festival village.

14:30 - 16:00: Chill Vibes Acoustic Stage

Head over to the Chill Vibes Stage for a serene interlude of acoustic and indie performances. Take a break from the slopes and immerse yourself in the soulful melodies and intimate vibes of talented artists.

16:30 - 18:00: Winter Sports Competitions

Watch in awe as top athletes showcase their skills in the skiing and snowboarding championships. The energy on the slopes is contagious, and you'll be on the edge of your seat as competitors navigate the challenging courses.

18:30 - 20:00: Sunset Jam Session

As the sun begins to dip below the mountain peaks, gather at the Main Stage for a magical sunset jam session. Enjoy performances from emerging artists and soak in the vibrant hues of the winter sky.

20:30 - 00:00: Nighttime Revelry

The grand finale awaits as internationally acclaimed headliners take the stage. Dance under the stars to electrifying beats, surrounded by the snowy landscape. The festival reaches its peak as the night sky comes alive with a spectacular fireworks display, marking the culmination of a day filled with music, adventure, and the spirit of winter.
