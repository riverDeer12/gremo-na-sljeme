Dear Festival Enthusiasts,

Words of welcome

As the organizers of the "Gremo na Sljeme" Ski Music Festival, we extend a warm welcome to all our fellow winter adventurers and music enthusiasts. This festival is a celebration of the magical union between the thrill of skiing on the stunning slopes of Sljeme and the pulsating beats of live music echoing through the snow-covered peaks.

Our journey began with a vision to create a unique experience where the beauty of winter sports intertwines with the excitement of a music festival. Over the years, "Gremo na Sljeme" has grown from a modest gathering to a globally recognized event, attracting top-tier artists and a diverse community of festival-goers.

This year, our commitment to delivering an extraordinary experience is stronger than ever. The festival grounds will not only host exhilarating skiing and snowboarding sessions but also showcase the talents of incredible musicians, creating an atmosphere of pure bliss in the midst of the winter wonderland.

We invite you to immerse yourself in the festivities, from the adrenaline-pumping winter sports competitions to the soulful acoustic performances on the Chill Vibes Stage and the grandeur of the international headliners under the starlit sky.

Your safety, enjoyment, and the preservation of our beautiful surroundings are our top priorities. We've meticulously planned every detail to ensure that your experience at "Gremo na Sljeme" is unforgettable and sustainable.

Thank you for being a part of this incredible journey. Whether you're a seasoned festival-goer or joining us for the first time, we're excited to share this adventure with you. Let's make memories, create connections, and embrace the spirit of winter together.

See you on the slopes!

Best Regards,
The Organizing Team
